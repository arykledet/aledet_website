About Me

Hello, my name is Aryk (pronounced Ah-ryk) and I am an embeddded systems engineer that takes on problems using a first principles approach.

  • I am currently working as an assistant staff at MIT Lincoln Laboratory in the Advanced Systems and Capabilities Group
  • I am a co-instructor for the MIT Beaver Works Summer Institute Autonomous Air Vehicle Racing course
  • I am the instructor for the LLRISE Python course
  • I graduated as Summa Cum Laude with my B.S. in EE from Johnson & Wales University
  • I was the chair and co-founder of the Johnson & Wales University ACM chapter
  • I was a co-founder and programmer for the Johnson & Wales University VEX Robotics club
  • I was a team member of the SkillsUSA robotics team at William M. Davies Carrier and Tech High School
  • Some of my non-technical hobbies include hiking, cycling, photography, basketball, and painting.
  • Not a web designer
